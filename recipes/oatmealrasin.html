Oatmeal Rasin Cookies

Description

This Oatmeal Rasin cookie recipe is simply the best there is. Something about the plump rasins, chewy texture, and soft centers is truly magical. Sweetened with brown sugar, loaded with oats and rasins. This recipe is a tried-and-true classic dessert.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened to room temperature
  • 1 cup packed light or dark brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 tablespoon pure vanilla extract (yes, Tablespoon!)
  • 1 tablespoon unsulphured or dark molasses
  • 1 and 1/2 cups all purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 and 1/2 teaspoons ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 3 cups old fashioned whole rolled oats
  • 1 cup rasins
  • optional: 1/2 cup chopped toasted walnuts
