Rhubarb Pie

Description

This sweet and tangy Rhubarb Pie recipe holds together very nicely. With just five ingredients, this simple, yet delicious recipe is an absolute show stopper.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/3 cups white sugar
  • 6 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1 (14.1 ounce) package double-crust pie pastry, thawed
  • 4 cups chopped rhubarb
  • 1 tablespoon butter
